# Lore

## About

**Lore** is an intelligent, AI-powered knowledge management platform designed specifically for engineering and operations teams. It serves as your organization's continuous memory, automatically capturing and transforming daily workflows into a searchable, trusted knowledge base.

Instead of writing documentation after the fact, Lore seamlessly integrates with your existing developer tools to make documentation a byproduct of your work:

- **Capture Critical Context:** Save Slack threads on demand, record terminal learnings directly from the CLI, and auto-ingest GitHub PR discussions before they disappear. 
- **Build Living Runbooks:** Turn scattered operational knowledge, incident responses, and deployment logs into working, verified runbooks that your team can review, publish, and trust.
- **AI-Powered Search & Grounding:** Give your teammates and AI agents the exact same context. When someone asks about a deployment, an incident, or a database migration, Lore provides grounded answers with the original source material attached.

Lore eliminates knowledge silos by connecting **Slack, GitHub, and the CLI** into a unified organizational memory. Whether you're handling a database failover, drafting an incident response, or sharing a complex terminal command, Lore ensures that the moments worth keeping are preserved and instantly accessible.
